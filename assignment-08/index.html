What in Tarnation is a Swing Beat?

If you're one of the wonderful people in this world who can read sheet music, count out the beats, and identify a waltz beat vs a swing beat vs whatever other beats there are: leave my site and get off my lawn.
For the rest of us: here's a quick summary of what the above people mean when they say "swing beat".

Music with a swing beat is music where the second and fourth beats in a 4/4 measure played in 8th notes are "swung", which means slightly delayed.* This means that instead of nice uniform beat beat beat beat the measure sounds more like beat be-beat beat be-beat†. Music people will try to count out one and-a-two three and-a-four. It's fun to let them explain once or twice, but once you get past the 20 minute mark both you and the music person will become distressed.
